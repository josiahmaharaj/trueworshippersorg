Our Vision

To lead people to a vital and personal relationship with Jesus Christ. To help them grow and mature so they might join in the work of the great commisson that we may affect the world, on person, one family and one community at a time.

Our Mission

To see people genuinely committed to Jesus Christ, committed to building up His body and as a church family be committed to reaching this world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Goals

Reaching the world with the Gospel by being a training, sending, supporting mody - by establishing churhes as the Holy Spirit directs and by parenting those works.


Reaching the lost locally with the Gospel through the community services(invitations/ outreach meeting -open air-crusades / visitations / home study programs/ ministry, through camps, sport and other adult and teen activities


Provide Pastoral Ministry - loving, caring, tending, and restoring by hand-on pastoral care from Pastors & Elders


Teaching Ministry - providing biblical, spiritual, relevant teaching thriugh relevent Sunday morning servies, Bible Studies, new believers Classes and Sound Christian education for every age.

What we Believe in

We Believe that there is one God, eternally existent in the Persons of the Holy Trinity as three eternal distinctions. Father, Son and Holy Ghost, Complete Unity in Trinity and Trinity in Unity, in one divine essence. (1 Corinthians 8:6; 1 John 5:7)

We Believe in the Eternal, Immutable, Omniscient, Omnipotent, Omnipresent God, the Creator of the Universe. (Genesis 1:1)

We Believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, the eternal Son of God, who before His incarnation, preexisted with God and was God. He was conceived of the Holy Spirit and is Totally God and Totally man. (John 1:1,2,14)

We Believe in the virgin birth of the Lord Jesus Christ, His unqualified deity, His sinless humanity and perfect life, the eternal all—sufficiency of His atoning death, His bodily resurrection, His ascension to the Father’s right hand.(Jn. 20:20,25-29; Rom. 1 :4)

We Believe in the death, burial and resurrection of Jesus Christ who became the sin offering for us that we may receive the gift of salvation. (Matthew 28:6; 1 Corinthians 15:4-9)

We Believe that Jesus ascended into heaven and is now seated at the right hand of the Father making intercession for us as our High Priest and Advocate while we await His imminent return. (Acts 1:8-11; Hebrews 4:14-16)

We Believe that through the grace of the Lord Jesus and the love of the Father all who receive Him, by faith, are born again of the Holy Spirit and shall have everlasting life. (John 1:12-13 & 3:16; Ephesians 2:8-9; Titus 3:5)

We Believe in Christ’s Lordship of the Church, the observance of the ordinances of Christian Baptism by Immersion for believers and the Lord’s Supper. (Acts 6 1-6,1 Cor. 11 : 23 foll.)

We Believe that the Holy Spirit, the third person of the Trinity, is God .He is a person who possesses intellect, emotion and will. (Gen. 1:2;John 14:16; Eph.4:30.)

We Believe that His work is to convict, regenerate, seal and indwell everyone who accepts Jesus Christ as Saviour, imparting all spiritual gifts for the edification of the body. (Romans 8:9 & 12:6-8)

We Believe that justification is a judicial act of God on the believer’s behalf solely on the merits of Christ, and that regeneration by the power of the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential for personal salvation.

WE We Believe in holy living, the present day reality of Baptism in the Holy Spirit with signs following, the Gifts of the Holy Spirit and the Lord’s supernatural healing of the human body.(Acts 2.,1Cor. 12-14)

We Believe the Holy Scriptures to be the divinely inbreathed, infallible, inerrant and authoritative Word of God. (2Tim. 3:16)

We Believe in the eternal blessedness of the redeemed in heaven and the eternal doom of the unregenerate in the lake of fire. (1 Thess. 4 : 13 – 18; Rev. 20 : 15)
